- Reynold's Transport Theorem
    - 

      $$B_{sys} = \int \beta dV$$

      
    - 

      $$B = \frac{dB}{dm}$$

      
    - 

      $$\frac{dB}{dt} = \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \int_{CV} \beta \rho dV + \int_{A_e} \beta_e \rho_e V_e dA - \int_{A_i} \beta_i \rho_i V_i dA$$

      
    - where B is any extensive property (could be mass, momentum, etc.)
