    - 

      $$\Delta u = c_{ave}\Delta T$$

      
    - 

      $$\Delta h = \Delta u + \nu \Delta p$$

      
    - Cases (States) for Change in Enthalpy>>>
        - Solids: 

          $$\Delta h = \Delta u$$

          
        - Liquids:
            - Constant Pressure: $\Delta h = \Delta u$
            - Constant Temperature: $\Delta h = \nu \Delta p$
